What We Offer

Barbecue Charcoal Briquette

Our Barbecue Charcoal Briquettes are precisely manufactured from a premium blend of coconut shell charcoal and natural hardwood, compressed into uniform, pillow-shaped or hexagonal blocks. The consistent shape and density of each briquette ensures even air circulation and reliable, predictable heat throughout the entire grilling session.

Designed for both home grillers and commercial BBQ operations, our briquettes ignite easily, build heat steadily, and sustain a high, even temperature for significantly longer than conventional lump charcoal. With very low smoke output and no unpleasant chemical smell, they are ideal for open-air cooking, restaurants, catering services, and outdoor events of any scale.

Our BBQ Charcoal Briquettes are produced under strict quality controls and are free from paraffin, harmful chemicals, or artificial additives. They comply with international food safety standards and are export-ready for markets in the Middle East, Europe, Australia, and beyond. Custom branding and private label packaging are available for wholesale buyers.

Product Specifications

Moisture Content: ≤ 6%
Ash Content: ≤ 6%
Fixed Carbon: ≥ 70%
Calorific Value: ≥ 6,800 kcal/kg
Shape: Pillow / Hexagonal (customizable)
Burn Time: 3–4 hours
