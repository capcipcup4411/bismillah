What We Offer

Hardwood Charcoal

Our Hardwood Charcoal is crafted from carefully selected premium hardwood species sourced from sustainably managed forests across Indonesia. The wood undergoes a controlled carbonization process in modern kilns, producing a dense, high-quality charcoal that is free from bark, impurities, and chemical treatments.

Known for its natural, authentic wood aroma and clean burn, our Hardwood Charcoal is a top choice for professional chefs, restaurant operators, and BBQ enthusiasts. It lights quickly, maintains a stable and high heat for extended periods, and produces very little smoke — allowing the true flavors of the food to shine through.

Available in lump form and suitable for both domestic and commercial use, our Hardwood Charcoal can be supplied in flexible packaging options to match your operational needs. We handle both small and large-volume orders with consistent quality across every shipment.

Product Specifications

Moisture Content: ≤ 6%
Ash Content: ≤ 5%
Fixed Carbon: ≥ 72%
Calorific Value: ≥ 6,500 kcal/kg
Form: Lump charcoal
Packaging: 3 kg, 5 kg, 10 kg, or custom
