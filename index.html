Who We Are

About Us

PT Karsa Jaya Karya is an Indonesian-based company engaged in the production and export of high-quality charcoal products. Founded with a commitment to delivering reliable and sustainable energy solutions, we focus on producing premium charcoal sourced from natural and renewable materials. Every stage of our production process is carefully managed to ensure consistent quality, efficiency, and performance for both domestic and international markets.

Through continuous improvement and strong operational standards, PT Karsa Jaya Karya has developed a growing presence in the global market, supplying charcoal products to various international destinations, particularly across the Middle East. We prioritize product excellence, dependable partnerships, and long-term customer satisfaction.

Our mission is to provide environmentally responsible energy solutions that comply with international quality standards while contributing to environmental sustainability and supporting local communities. PT Karsa Jaya Karya is dedicated to powering industries and households through sustainable energy for the future.

What We Offer

Our Products

Explore our premium coconut charcoal briquettes and sustainable products tailored for your needs.

Coconut Shell Charcoal

Premium charcoal produced from natural coconut shells. Known for high heat output, long burning time, and low ash content. Suitable for shisha, grilling, and industrial applications.

Hardwood Charcoal

Made from selected natural hardwood, providing stable heat and consistent performance. Easy to ignite with long-lasting burn, ideal for restaurants, barbecue use, and commercial cooking.

Coconut Charcoal Briquette

High-density briquettes made from compressed coconut shell charcoal. Delivers steady heat, minimal smoke, and low ash residue. Commonly used for shisha and premium heating purposes.

Barbecue Charcoal Briquette

Uniform charcoal briquettes designed for efficient grilling. Provides even heat distribution, longer burning duration, and reliable performance for household and commercial barbecue needs.
