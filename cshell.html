What We Offer

Coconut Shell Charcoal

Our Coconut Shell Charcoal is produced from 100% natural coconut shells sourced from the best quality coconuts in Indonesia. The production process uses no additives or chemical binders, ensuring a pure, clean-burning product that meets the strictest international quality standards.

With its exceptionally high carbon content, this charcoal is widely recognized for its superior heat output, extended burn time, and remarkably low ash production. It generates minimal smoke and virtually no unpleasant odor, making it the preferred choice for shisha cafés, premium restaurants, and large-scale industrial operations across the Middle East, Europe, and Asia.

Our Coconut Shell Charcoal is available in various sizes and can be customized to meet specific customer requirements. Each batch is thoroughly tested and certified to ensure consistent quality in every shipment.

Product Specifications

Moisture Content: ≤ 5%
Ash Content: ≤ 3%
Fixed Carbon: ≥ 78%
Calorific Value: ≥ 7,000 kcal/kg
Burn Time: 2–3 hours
Packaging: 1 kg, 3 kg, 5 kg, 10 kg, or custom
